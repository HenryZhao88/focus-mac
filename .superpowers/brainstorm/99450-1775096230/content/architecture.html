Data Flow
1. You pick a task → tap Start Focus
2. App Monitor + Tab Watcher stream your activity to the AI Core every few seconds
3. AI decides: on-task or drifting?
4. Drift detected → gentle nudge overlay appears
5. Ignored → escalate (overlay covers the off-task window)
6. You can ask the AI to unlock something → it adds to session allowlist
